QUESTIONS

Before you get in touch.

Is Acceptor's platform PCI DSS compliant?

Yes — Acceptor is PCI DSS Level 1 compliant, the highest security standard in the payments industry, across every transaction type on the platform.

What does it cost to get started?

Zero-cost EFTPOS or flat-rate from 1.2% — no monthly account fees, no lock-in contracts. See both options above.

How long does onboarding actually take?

Typically around 5 business days from application to live terminal, including hardware dispatch and platform configuration.

What if I already have a payment provider?

We manage the switch end-to-end — hardware, integration and account setup — with no disruption to trading while you move across.

Is my statement and transaction data safe?

Yes — all data is handled under the same PCI DSS Level 1 controls that secure every transaction on the platform.

What payment types does Acceptor support?

Card-present, online checkout, recurring billing, BNPL, digital wallets, multi-currency and invoicing — all on one platform, with full reporting across every channel.
